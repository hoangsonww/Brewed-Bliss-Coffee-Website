About Brewed Bliss

At Brewed Bliss, we believe that every cup of coffee tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Chapel Hill, our cozy coffee shop provides a welcoming space where friends gather, conversations flow, and coffee takes center stage. We are dedicated to providing our customers with expertly crafted coffee, ethically sourced from the best farms around the world.

Our mission goes beyond coffee – it's about bringing people together in a space where they can unwind, connect, and enjoy moments of bliss. Whether you’re here for a quiet study session or a catch-up with friends, Brewed Bliss is your place to relax, recharge, and enjoy a cup made just for you.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the finest coffee beans, roasted to perfection for a rich, flavorful experience.
  • Community: Brewed Bliss is a place where friendships grow, conversations spark, and connections are made.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly practices and partner with farms committed to sustainable coffee cultivation.
  • Passion: Our baristas are artists, crafting each cup with creativity, care, and expertise.

Meet Our Team

Maria Nguyen

Head Barista

Maria brings over a decade of coffee experience and a great passion for creating the perfect coffee cup. Her expertise in brewing, roasting, and latte art makes her a favorite among our customers. Maria's signature drink, the "Blissful Brew," is also a must-try!

Jared Smith

Baker & Pastry Chef

Jared's fresh pastries are the perfect complement to any cup of coffee. With a background in artisan baking, he fills our shop with the scent of croissants, muffins, and more. His signature almond croissant has become a local favorite in Chapel Hill!

Katherine Chen

Barista

Known for her latte art skills, Katherine loves bringing beauty to every cup she serves. She’s always experimenting with new designs and flavors to surprise and delight our customers, so her creativity never stops!
